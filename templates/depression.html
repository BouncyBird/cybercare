Depression

Things to try:

  • Get in a routine. Planning out your day will let you get more done, instead of sorrowing over any pain that you may have.
  • Try new things, like finding a new way to get to work.
  • Try rearranging furniture. New design can lead to a new you.
  • Express yourself with writing. Whether it is online or in a notebook, keep a diary and note how you feel.
  • Keep a list of pros and strengths in your life to help show you what an amazing person you are. When you feel depressed, you may think that everything is terrible, and may think it's your or someone you know’s fault. Show yourself how amazing you are.
  • Stay connected with friends and family as socializing is one of the best ways to release endorphins and dopamine, making you feel relaxed.

Meditation:

  • lie down.
  • take deep slow breaths
  • concentrate on the air coming in and out of your lungs as you close your eyes
  • Sink into where you are laying as you lose feeling throughout your body.
  • Don’t move and concentrate on on your limbs. Any twitching, and tensing
  • One by one tense and untense your body one part at a time starting at the toes, Ending at the top of your head.
  • When you are fully up continue your deep breaths and stretch.
  • When you are done with your stretches, you are done with this meditation routine.
